BE GREEK

Be Greek is based on the concept of providing to the visitor the true and authentic experience of Greece.

EXPERIENCES

The visitor through semi-tailored tours can get a glimpse of the Greek culture, via flavours, smells and interactions with the locals in untouched from massive tourism areas.

AUTHENTICITY

Our experienced and friendly tour managers are setting the scenes for once in a life-time experiences. Visitors are invited to join our private groups in an adventure that will remember for ever. Join our daily tours and get ready for real and authentic moments in Greece.

Company Bio

We are a team of college students working on this project like it's our full time job. Any amount would help support and continue development on this project and is greatly appreciated.
